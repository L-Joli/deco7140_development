GenAI Acknowledgement

How GenAI Was Used

I used ChatGPT in multiple ways throughout this assignment to support both the research and design process. I conducted interviews with the AI to practice user research, which allowed me to explore how a user might respond to various concerns. After identifying the client’s values, I brainstormed potential features with ChatGPT, helping me expand and refine my ideas. During development, I asked ChatGPT to generate an HTML template for presenting my data and to refine my web design style. Additionally, I used it for grammar checking, generating a reset.css file, creating a navigation bar, improving accessibility, and developing helper CSS classes.

How GenAI Supported Learning

Using ChatGPT supported my learning in several ways. It helped me understand concepts more clearly, particularly in phrasing questions for user research, which allowed me to expand my ideas and thoughts. It also sped up my web development and design process, giving me more time to focus on learning new concepts. Through AI assistance, I discovered new approaches for addressing accessibility requirements on a web page and explored techniques for improving usability. Additionally, asking ChatGPT for clarification on client values and user needs strengthened my understanding and confidence in applying these concepts effectively.

GenAI’s Effectiveness

ChatGPT was most helpful for styling HTML and addressing accessibility requirements. However, some style fine-tuning suggestions didn’t work as expected, so I often needed to adjust the results myself. Even when it provided code, I had to double-check its output to ensure it didn’t unintentionally alter my ideas or wording. Overall, ChatGPT enhanced my thinking, particularly during brainstorming, by allowing me to explore ideas independently and ask follow-up questions to clarify concepts. At times, if my prompts were not specific enough, the AI struggled to understand my requirements, such as for creating the navigation bar, so I needed to iterate multiple times and make manual adjustments.

Ethical Considerations

I made sure to clearly distinguish my own ideas from AI suggestions and kept track of all usage in a GenAI acknowledgment form. I double-checked the AI’s contributions to ensure it did not unintentionally alter my ideas, and I used Git to monitor changes. To maintain accuracy and responsibility, I tested the usability of AI-generated code and cross-referenced suggestions with official documentation. Finally, I ensured that my use of ChatGPT aligned with academic guidelines by using it as a supportive tool rather than a replacement for my own thinking, making sure all final decisions and ideas were my own.
